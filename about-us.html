About GemPages !

2017

Building an eCommerce store used to be expensive, complex and difficult for retail merchants, especially SMEs. Well-aware of the challenges after spending years working with worldwide eSellers, our founder Chris began writing the very first code for GemPages - a platform that simplifies and visualizes web design for all.
